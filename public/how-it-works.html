How citation verification works

The system first cleans and splits pasted text, then searches trusted academic metadata sources, compares fields, assigns confidence, and flags suspicious or unverifiable references.

01

Paste repair

Built-in local AI repairs broken DOI links, compact author-year text, page-number runs, and PDF/OCR line breaks.

02

Metadata search

The app checks Crossref, DOI.org, OpenAlex, PubMed, Europe PMC, arXiv, DataCite, Semantic Scholar, Google Books, and Open Library.

03

Risk scoring

Each reference receives status, confidence, hallucination risk, corrected reference, and an expandable evidence report.
