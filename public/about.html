About Cite Validator

Cite Validator helps researchers, students, editors, teachers, and publishers verify references before publication or submission. It is designed to be transparent: uncertain citations stay uncertain until trusted metadata supports them.

Purpose

Reference validation

The app checks titles, authors, publication years, journal or conference names, publishers, volume, issue, pages, DOI, ISBN, ISSN, PMID, and arXiv identifiers across broad public metadata sources.

Risk

Citation hallucination detection

It classifies references as verified, partially verified, suspicious, likely hallucinated or fabricated, or unverifiable.

Access

Totally free

Cite Validator is free to use and built to make academic reference checking more accessible.
